Who are we?

We are ambitious high school and college students actively contributing to the excellence of Benicia's academic environment. Each of our tutors has sizable experience in academics, from enrollment in multiple AP (Advanced Placement)classes to college level educational programs.

What do we do?

We offer casual at-home in-person or virtual tutoring sessions for high school and middle school students. Our main focus is math, but please contact us if you seek tutoring in other academic areas; we will still be able to help.
